YOGA

Meet Your Guide:
Sarah Jenkins

With over 15 years of dedicated practice and 10 years of teaching experience, Sarah is passionate about making yoga accessible and transformative for everyone. Her approach blends traditional wisdom with modern understanding, emphasizing compassionate self-exploration.

Specializing in Vinyasa, Restorative, and Meditation practices, Sarah guides students to cultivate strength, flexibility, and profound inner peace. She believes yoga is not just a physical exercise, but a pathway to holistic well-being and a joyful life.
